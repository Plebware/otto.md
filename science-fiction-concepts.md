# 🌌 Introduction

Welcome to the fictional worlds of **CyberCat** and **Armadillo** — a blend of science fiction, spiritual allegory, military realism, railway grit, cyberpunk philosophy, and Christian symbolism.

These stories are not traditional science fiction.

They are **journeys of endurance**, survival, sacrifice, loyalty, and redemption told through futuristic worlds inspired by:

- 🚂 Railway engineering
- 🪖 Military discipline
- ⚡ Cybernetic futures
- 🛰️ Deep-space exploration
- ✝️ Christian allegory
- 🔥 Spiritual warfare
- 🧠 Psychological endurance
- 🌍 Post-collapse civilizations

# 🐈 CyberCat Series

## ⚡ Who is CyberCat?

CyberCat is a mysterious cybernetic feline operative inspired by a real-world companion cat named Taylor.

He exists somewhere between:

- machine 🤖
- survivor 🩸
- scout 🛰️
- and spiritual witness ✝️

CyberCat navigates ruined megacities, abandoned orbital stations, war-torn colonies, and forgotten rail systems stretching across planets.
