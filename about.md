# About Otto

## Otto Brinkmeier

Welcome to the Otto Archive.

I am a South African writer, Linux enthusiast, system experimenter, and creator of the PlebMachine project.

My work combines technical exploration, creative writing, philosophical reflection, and practical problem solving.

Over the years I have worked in multiple disciplines, including railway electrical maintenance, mechanical diagnostics, electronics, computing, and independent creative projects.

Those experiences shaped the practical engineering philosophy that now drives both my writing and technical work.

## The PlebMachine Project

PlebMachine began as an evolving exploration into modular Linux orchestration.

The project focuses on:

- human-centered computing
- state-driven workflows
- old hardware usability
- lightweight desktop control
- practical automation
- recoverability and maintainability

Rather than building around hype or complexity, the philosophy behind PlebMachine is rooted in simplicity, transparency, and adaptability.

## Development Philosophy

I believe technology should remain understandable, useful, and human.

Modern systems often become unnecessarily bloated, fragile, and disconnected from the people using them.

My work attempts to move in the opposite direction:

- lighter systems
- clearer workflows
- modular design
- long-term maintainability
- practical engineering
